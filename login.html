Welcome to Inventory Manager

Praxair Express lets you search our inventory, place orders, and check order status - all online. Customers with an established account with Praxair Distribution, Inc. can order gases, equipment & accessories. If you do not have an established account, use the Credit Card Only Registration option to browse and order equipment & accessories right away. Gases will not be available to you for purchase, but you can contact your local sales office for more information or to open an account.

With Praxair Express you can create templates with our "custom order" feature or have Praxair Express create them for you. Either way, repeat ordering is made quick and easy. With Praxair Express you get the tools you need to make you and your business more productive.Learn more about Praxair Express.
